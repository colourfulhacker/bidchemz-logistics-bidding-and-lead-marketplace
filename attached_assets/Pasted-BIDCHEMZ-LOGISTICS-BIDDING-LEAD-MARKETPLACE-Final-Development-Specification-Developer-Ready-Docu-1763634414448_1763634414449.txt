BIDCHEMZ LOGISTICS BIDDING & LEAD MARKETPLACE

Final Development Specification (Developer-Ready Document)
Version: 1.0

1. BUSINESS MODEL
1.1 Summary

The platform enables reverse-bidding for chemical logistics:

A trader submits a freight requirement.

The system matches eligible logistics partners.

Partners submit competitive price quotations.

Trader compares all offers and selects one.

The selected partner is charged a lead fee.

Bidchemz generates revenue through lead monetization.

1.2 Core Objectives

Simplify logistics procurement for chemical traders.

Deliver high-quality, capability-matched leads to logistics partners.

Provide a scalable, API-driven logistics engine integrated with the chemical marketplace.

Monetize logistics via transparent lead pricing and partner tiers.

Trader (Buyer/Seller): Requests logistics support and selects offers.

9. MARKETPLACE API INTEGRATION
Mandatory API Endpoints

POST /api/v1/quotes – Create logistics request

GET /api/v1/quotes/{id} – Retrieve request details

Shipment tracking endpoints
